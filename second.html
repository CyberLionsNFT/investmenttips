Bill Gates On A Mission To Change The World And Help Ordinary People Get Out Of Poverty

"I have no personal use for money anymore, so I entirely dedicated my time to help back humanity" Bill explains. "That's why I invested in the startup which primary purpose is to push shift towards universal income society. It's still in its beta phase, but people can already apply in several countries where we have business set. Though, spots are limited."

Martha Brant

Appearing on the 'This Morning' show, Bill Gates, founder of Microsoft revealed that he recently invested £19 million in a new revolutionary startup called . The whole idea behind is to allow the average person the opportunity to make money from home. Even if they have never made a trade in their life before or even if they are not tech-savvy as it's all being done with the smartphone.

The official website of claims it can generate high profits for any user by executing a unique trading algorithm. This algorithm enables the user to automatically trade Bitcoin and other crypto currencies.

In order to start the fully automated process, user needs to deposit an initial amount of £200. The algorithm would then automatically choose the perfect time to buy Bitcoin low and sell it high - and vice versa. This way the user profits would always be maximized without any risk.

As of few days ago they've started accepting registrations from United Kingdom as well, but not for long it seems, due to very high demand.

Bill said that any credit card can be used for the initial deposit.

We Decided To Investigate

If is constantly making money for its users, how does the company earn any money?

We found out that the answer is simple but genius: keeps exactly 8% of the users' profits. In other words, only when the user makes money, they makes money. This explains why they are so 'motivated' to generate as much profits for their users as possible.

It says on their website that in order to activate the account and start making money, the user would have to deposit a minimum of £200 GBP ($250 dollars).

Currently there are no exchange fees or reductions from the deposit. As soon as the deposit is made, the account and trading algorithm are activated.
